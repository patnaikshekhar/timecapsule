How it works

Upload

Upload pictures on this app. You can also add a title and description to the photo, describing the event. This will help to look back and remember at past memories

Set date

Set a date and time when you want the time capsule to be sent. There is no hassle of storing your data on this app.

Send

Put in the email of the person you want to send the capsule to. The time capsule will be automatically sent to the recipient atoumatically depending on the set time and date
